Firebase Invites Plugin Demo

The Firebase Invites plugin allows your users to leverage their Google account to send invites to contacts on their phone by E-mail or SMS.

Custom plugins don't work in the simulator and the AppBuilder companion app, so inside Telerik AppBuilder choose Run - Build - iOS/Android - App package.
